Does it feel warm in here?

By: l-burrell

Okay folks, the instructions are simple!

TIP 1: You must guess a number from 1 to 100.

TIP 2: You must watch the color to determine if you are hot or cold.

TIP 3: When it is a deep red, you are on fire! And close to winning!

TIP 4: When it is blue, you are far away from the correct answer.

TIP 5: If you attempt to make the same guess a second time, it wont work!
